Anguilla Day 2

July 14, 2007February 29, 2012 Steven A. Cholewiak Personal, Photos

Pictures from my second day on Anguilla.

Continue to the post for more pictures.


There were lizards EVERYWHERE.

The next stop was Upper Shoal Bay.

On the drive back, we passed a group of laborers on strike. My friend’s father told me about how they were brought to Anguilla under false pretenses and the horrible conditions that they lived in. This was the most unfortunate and upsetting scene during my entire trip.

After returning, we decided to grab some margaritas. Delicious.
